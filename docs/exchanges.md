## Purpose

---

## Important Warning

For very small or low-liquidity assets, a listed market does **not** automatically mean:

- safe trading
- meaningful liquidity
- easy withdrawals
- fair price discovery

Always verify:
- order book depth
- withdrawals
- deposits
- actual spread
- recent trade activity
